Key Findings — strategic insights
🎯
ICP is clearer than expected
Mid-size agencies (10–50 people) with multiple client accounts are the clearest fit. They have the pain, budget, and urgency — but don't have the resources to build internal AI tools.
ICP CONFIRMED
💡
Use case is "client servicing layer"
Not content creation — agencies want Marc.AI to handle the structural, repeatable work: briefs, onboarding decks, monthly reports, strategy templates. The value is time reclaimed.
USE CASE SHARP
⚡
Keynote created inbound pull
Zero cold outreach was needed. Wendy's presentation of "My New Flow" made the use case tangible — people saw themselves in the problem. This is a replicable acquisition motion.
ACQUISITION INSIGHT
⚠️
Pricing expectations unclear
No one asked about price — which means we don't yet know what they'd pay. This is the biggest open question before moving to demo stage. Need to validate pricing range in follow-ups.
